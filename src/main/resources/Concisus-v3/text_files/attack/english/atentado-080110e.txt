The Togo national football team bus attack was a terrorist attack on the team bus of the Togo national football team that occurred on 8 January 2010 as the team traveled through the Angolan province of Cabinda on the way to the 2010 Africa Cup of Nations tournament, which began on 10 January.Bus driver Mário Adjoua, the teams assistant manager Abalo Amelete, and media officer Stanislas Ocloo were killed, with several others injured.
