The 1999 Izmit earthquake, also known as the Kocaeli earthquake or the Gölcük earthquake, was a 7.6 magnitude earthquake that struck northwestern Turkey on August 17, 1999, at about 3:02am local time. The event lasted for 37 seconds, killing around 17,000 people and leaving approximatel 500000 people homeless. The nearby city of Izmit was very badly damaged.
