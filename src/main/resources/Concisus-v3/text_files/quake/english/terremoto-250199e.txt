The 1999 Coffee Axis, Colombia earthquake was an earthquake that heavily affected the city of Armenia, Colombia in the Quindío department, 18 towns and 28 villages in the Colombian Coffee-Growers Axis region departments, and to a lesser degree, the cities of Pereira and Manizales.  The earthquake occurred on Monday, 25 January 1999 13:19 (18:19 UTC) and had a magnitude of 6.2. The epicenter was 25 miles (40 km) west south west of Ibague, Colombia.
